What I've Learned
My Tips
1
👀
Mirror, Signal, Manoeuvre — always check your mirrors before doing anything. MSM every single time.
2
🐢
Slow down early — approach junctions and roundabouts slower than you think. You can always speed up.
3
🔄
Roundabouts — give way to the right, signal when leaving, stay calm and look early.
4
⚙️
Gears & speed — match your gear to your speed. Under 10mph = 1st. Don't ride the clutch.
5
🧠
Stay calm — if you make a mistake, breathe and carry on. Everyone makes them.
6
📺
Watch videos — Conquer Driving on YouTube explains everything better than anyone.
